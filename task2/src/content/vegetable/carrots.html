carrots

How to grow :

  1. 1. Choose Varieties: Select organic carrot varieties like Nantes, Danvers, or Chantenay.
  2. 2. Prepare Soil: Ensure loose, well-drained soil with a pH of 6.0-6.8, adding compost for fertility.
  3. 3. Sow Seeds: Plant seeds 1/4 inch deep in rows 12-18 inches apart, spacing 2 inches apart within rows.
  4. 4. Thin Seedlings: Thin to 2-4 inches apart once seedlings are 2 inches tall. Water Consistently Keep soil moist, watering deeply once a week.
  5. 5. Fertilize: Use compost or balanced organic fertilizer if necessary, avoiding high-nitrogen fertilizers.
  6. 6. Harvest: Pull carrots 70-80 days after planting, loosening soil first.

Health Benefits :

Carrots benefit health by improving vision, immunity, heart health, skin health, aiding digestion, and providing essential nutrients and antioxidants.
